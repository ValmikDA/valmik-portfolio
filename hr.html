📊 HR Analytics Case Study

📌 Problem

High employee attrition impacting business performance.

🔍 Approach

Used Tableau dashboards and KPI tracking for analysis.

📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

📈 Insights

  • Attrition driven by salary and job satisfaction
  • Early-stage employees most likely to leave
  • Low engagement correlates with high attrition

💡 Impact

Improved retention strategies and workforce planning.
