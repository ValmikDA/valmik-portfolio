📊 HR Attrition Case Study

📌 Problem

High employee attrition affecting business stability.

🔍 Approach

Analyzed HR data using Tableau dashboards and KPI tracking.

📊 Dashboard Visuals

📈 Key Insights

  • Attrition driven by low salary and job dissatisfaction
  • Higher churn in early-stage employees
  • Low engagement correlates with high attrition
  • Retention strategies needed for junior workforce

💡 Business Impact

Improved employee retention strategies and workforce planning.
