📊 HR Analytics Case Study

📌 Problem

High employee attrition was impacting productivity and increasing hiring costs. The goal was to identify key drivers of attrition and improve retention strategies.

📊 Data Overview

  • Employee ID
  • Age, Gender
  • Salary
  • Job Satisfaction
  • Years at Company
  • Attrition (Target Variable)

🎯 Objective

Identify patterns in employee attrition and provide actionable insights to improve retention.

🔍 Approach

  • Data cleaning and preparation
  • Exploratory Data Analysis
  • KPI creation (Attrition Rate, Retention Rate)
  • Dashboard visualization using Tableau

📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

📈 Insights

  • Attrition driven by low salary and job satisfaction
  • Early-career employees most likely to leave
  • Low engagement strongly correlates with attrition

💡 Business Impact

Enabled better workforce planning and targeted retention strategies, reducing attrition risk.
