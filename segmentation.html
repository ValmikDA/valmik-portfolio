🧩 Customer Segmentation Case Study

📌 Problem

Businesses often lack visibility into customer value, leading to inefficient marketing spend and poor targeting. The goal was to identify high-value customers and improve retention strategies.

📊 Data Overview

  • Customer ID
  • Purchase Frequency
  • Recency (last purchase)
  • Monetary Value (spend)
  • Return behavior

🎯 Objective

Segment customers based on behavior to improve targeting, retention, and lifecycle marketing.

🔍 Approach

  • Data cleaning and preprocessing
  • RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
  • K-means clustering (optimal clusters = 4)
  • Segment profiling and interpretation

📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

📈 Insights

  • Top 20% customers contribute majority revenue
  • Low-frequency users show high churn risk
  • High return-rate customers indicate dissatisfaction
  • Clear segmentation enables personalized campaigns

💡 Business Impact

Improved targeting, increased retention opportunities, and optimized marketing ROI through lifecycle-based segmentation.
