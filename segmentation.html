🧩 Customer Segmentation Case Study

📌 Problem

Business lacked visibility into high-value customers and retention opportunities.

🔍 Approach

Used K-means clustering and RFM analysis to segment customers.

📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

📈 Insights

  • Top 20% customers drive majority revenue
  • Low-frequency users show churn risk
  • Opportunity for retention campaigns
  • High return-rate customers need product improvement

💡 Impact

Enabled targeted marketing and improved customer retention strategies.
