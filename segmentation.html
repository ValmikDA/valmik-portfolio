🧩 Customer Segmentation Case Study

📌 Problem

Business lacked visibility into high-value customers and retention opportunities.

🔍 Approach

Used clustering (K-means) + RFM analysis to segment customers.

📊 Dashboard Visuals

📈 Key Insights

  • Top 20% customers drive majority of revenue
  • Low-frequency users show high churn risk
  • High return-rate segment needs product improvement
  • Opportunity for lifecycle campaigns to increase LTV

💡 Business Impact

Enabled targeted campaigns, improved retention strategy, and optimized marketing spend.
